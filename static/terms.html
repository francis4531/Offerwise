ARTICLE III

DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES AND PLATFORM

Section 3.01 Overview of Services

The Company provides an artificial intelligence-powered platform for analyzing real estate documents and generating property insights. The Platform utilizes advanced technologies including, but not limited to:

  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for document text extraction;
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for semantic understanding;
  • Machine Learning algorithms for pattern recognition and analysis;
  • Deep Learning neural networks for complex data relationships;
  • Proprietary AI models developed specifically for real estate analysis;
  • Cross-reference engines for comparing information across multiple documents;
  • Risk scoring algorithms for property condition assessment;
  • Predictive analytics for offer price recommendations.

Section 3.02 Specific Service Features

(a) Document Processing Services

The Platform provides automated processing of Real Estate Documents, including:

  • OCR Processing: Extraction of text from scanned or photographed documents, including typed and handwritten content (subject to legibility limitations);
  • Form Recognition: Identification and parsing of standard real estate forms, including inspection reports and disclosure forms;
  • Checkbox Detection: Recognition and interpretation of checkboxes, radio buttons, and other form elements;
  • Table Extraction: Parsing of tabular data and structured information;
  • Document Classification: Automatic categorization of uploaded documents by type and purpose;
  • Multi-Document Handling: Simultaneous processing of multiple related documents.

(b) Analysis Services

Upon successful document processing, the Platform generates Analysis Reports containing:

  • Property Risk DNA™: Comprehensive risk assessment across multiple categories including structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, environmental, and disclosure completeness;
  • OfferScore™: Algorithmic calculation of property desirability and investment potential;
  • Cross-Reference Analysis: Comparison of information between seller disclosures and inspection reports to identify discrepancies, omissions, or inconsistencies;
  • Seller Transparency Report™: Evaluation of disclosure completeness, accuracy, and seller forthrightness;
  • Offer Recommendations: AI-generated suggested offer price ranges based on property condition, market data, and risk factors;
  • Issue Prioritization: Ranking of identified problems by severity, cost, and urgency;
  • Repair Estimates: Approximate cost ranges for addressing identified issues (informational only);
  • Negotiation Insights: Strategic recommendations for price negotiations based on analysis findings.
