🔭 OfferWatch™

Every lead you claim automatically starts a 45-day background watch on that property — no setup required. Each morning at 7am PT, OfferWatch scans for permit filings, price drops, new comps, and seismic events. If the seller pulls permits for the same repair you quoted, or the listing price drops, you get an email immediately so you can act before the window closes.
