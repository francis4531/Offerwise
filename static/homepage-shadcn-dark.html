How it works

Three steps to a confident offer.

No real estate degree required. Upload your documents, wait 90 seconds, and walk into negotiations armed with data your agent doesn't have.

1
Upload your documents
Drop in your seller disclosure and inspection report. PDFs are parsed locally in your browser — we only receive extracted text. Your privacy is protected by design.
2
AI analyzes everything
We cross-reference 13 public data sources, calculate ZIP-adjusted repair costs, score the seller's transparency, and flag every contradiction between what was disclosed and what was found.
3
Get your number
Receive your recommended offer price, full repair breakdown, negotiation playbook, and a state-aware Request for Repair document ready to hand to your agent.
