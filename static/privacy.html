🔒 1. Document Privacy - We Don't Store Your Documents

Your property documents are NOT stored on our servers. This is the most important privacy feature of OfferWise:

What Happens to Your Documents:

  • Upload: When you upload seller disclosure statements or inspection reports
  • Processing: Documents are processed in-memory only to extract text for analysis
  • Analysis: Extracted text is analyzed by our OfferScore™ algorithm
  • Deletion: Documents and extracted text are automatically and permanently deleted immediately after your analysis completes
  • No Retention: We do not keep, archive, back up, or store any copies
  • No AI Training: Document content is never used to train AI models or for any purpose other than your specific analysis

Retention Timeline:

Data Type Retention Period
Uploaded Documents
(PDFs, files)		 0 seconds
Deleted immediately after processing
Extracted Text
(Document content)		 0 seconds
Deleted immediately after analysis
Analysis Results
(Scores, recommendations)		 Until account deletion
Stored in your analysis history

You are responsible for maintaining your own copies of all property documents. OfferWise cannot retrieve, provide, or recover documents once they are deleted.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (OAuth)

When you sign in using OAuth authentication, we collect:

  • Email Address: Your email from Google, Apple, or Facebook
  • Name: Your display name from your OAuth provider
  • OAuth Provider ID: Unique identifier from Google/Apple/Facebook
  • Profile Picture URL: If provided by your OAuth provider (optional)

We do NOT collect or store passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by your OAuth provider.

2.2 Analysis Data

We store metadata about your property analyses:

  • Property Address: As you entered it
  • Asking Price: The property price you provided
  • Analysis Results:
    • Overall risk score and risk tier (Low/Medium/High)
    • Category-specific risk scores (Foundation, Roof, etc.)
    • Repair cost estimates
    • Recommended offer price
    • Transparency score
  • Timestamp: When the analysis was performed

Note: We store analysis results, NOT the original documents or their full content.

2.3 Payment & Billing

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We collect and store:

  • Stripe Customer ID: Links your OfferWise account to Stripe
  • Purchase History: Dates, amounts, and credit packages purchased
  • Credit Balance: Number of available analysis credits

We do NOT store: Credit card numbers, CVV codes, or full payment card details. All payment information is securely stored and processed by Stripe.

2.4 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use OfferWise:

  • Login Activity: Timestamps of when you sign in
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and user agent
  • IP Address: Your internet protocol address
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system
  • Page Views: Which pages and features you access
  • Performance Data: Loading times, errors encountered

2.5 Buyer Profile (Optional)

If you create a buyer profile, we store:

  • Maximum budget
  • Repair tolerance level
  • Biggest concerns or regrets to avoid

This information is used to personalize your analysis recommendations.

4. Data Retention

4.1 Property Documents: ZERO RETENTION

Retention Period: 0 seconds

Uploaded seller disclosure statements and inspection reports are deleted immediately and permanently after your analysis is completed. There is no backup, no archive, and no recovery possible.

4.2 Analysis Results

Retention Period: Until account deletion

Your analysis history (risk scores, offer prices, property addresses, timestamps) is stored in your account indefinitely to provide you with access to your past analyses. You can:

  • View your analysis history in your Dashboard
  • Delete individual analyses at any time
  • Delete all analyses by deleting your account

4.3 Account Information

Retention Period: Until account deletion + 30 days

Your account data (email, name, OAuth ID) is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All account data is marked for deletion
  • Data is permanently removed from active systems within 30 days
  • Backups may retain data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

4.4 Payment Records

Retention Period: 7 years

For legal and tax compliance, we retain payment transaction records (purchase dates, amounts) for 7 years, even after account deletion. These records contain no payment card details.

4.5 Usage Logs

Retention Period: 90 days

Server logs (IP addresses, page views, timestamps) are retained for 90 days for security and troubleshooting, then automatically deleted.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

6.1 Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • At Rest: Database contents are encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Backups: All backups are encrypted

6.2 Access Controls

  • Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Administrative access requires multi-factor authentication
  • All access is logged and monitored

6.3 OAuth Security

  • No passwords stored on our servers
  • Authentication delegated to Google, Apple, Facebook
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and securely stored

6.4 Document Processing

  • Documents processed in-memory only
  • No temporary files written to disk
  • Immediate deletion after processing
  • No document content in logs or backups

6.5 Regular Security Audits

  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Vulnerability scanning
  • Security monitoring and alerting

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
