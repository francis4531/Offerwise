⚙️ Payment System Configuration Needed

The payment system is almost ready!
Your Stripe keys just need to be added to complete the setup.

For the site owner:
  1. Get your Stripe keys from: dashboard.stripe.com
  2. In Render dashboard, go to your web service
  3. Click "Environment" tab
  4. Add variable: STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY with your pk_test_... key
  5. Add variable: STRIPE_SECRET_KEY with your sk_test_... key
  6. Click "Save Changes" and wait for redeploy

Once configured, payments will process automatically through Stripe's secure platform.
