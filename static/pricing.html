Frequently Asked Questions

Do my credits expire?

No! Your analysis credits never expire. Buy them today and use them whenever you're ready, even years from now.

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes: AI-powered document parsing, cross-reference checking (disclosure vs inspection), risk scoring across 12 categories, detailed repair cost estimates, transparency analysis, and objective offer recommendations.

What if something goes wrong?

Quality Guarantee: If our analysis fails to process your documents due to a technical issue on our end, contact support@getofferwise.ai and we'll work with you to resolve it.

What if my documents aren't clear enough?

Our AI works best with typed, professional documents (PDFs). Scanned or handwritten documents may have limitations. Before purchasing, ensure your inspection report and disclosure forms are reasonably clear and complete. Contact us if you're unsure about document quality.

Can I get help if I have issues?

Absolutely! Email support@getofferwise.ai and we'll respond within 24 hours (usually much faster). We'll help troubleshoot document issues, re-run failed analyses, or answer any questions about your results.

Can I buy more analyses later?

Absolutely! You can purchase additional analyses at any time. Your credits stack and never expire, so you can build up your analysis balance whenever you want.

Is this better than a traditional home inspection?

OfferWise complements (doesn't replace) home inspections. Use OfferWise to analyze properties BEFORE making offers, then get a physical inspection on your finalist. At $16-29 per analysis vs $300-500 per inspection, you can analyze many properties affordably.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor.
