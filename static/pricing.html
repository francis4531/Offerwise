Frequently Asked Questions

Do my credits expire?

No! Your analysis credits never expire. Buy them today and use them whenever you're ready, even years from now.

What's included in each analysis?

Every analysis includes: AI-powered document parsing, cross-reference checking (disclosure vs inspection), risk scoring across 12 categories, detailed repair cost estimates, transparency analysis, and objective offer recommendations.

How does the free trial work?

Your first analysis is completely free with no credit card required. Simply upload your property documents and see the full analysis. If you love it (we think you will!), you can purchase more analyses.

Can I buy more analyses later?

Absolutely! You can purchase additional analyses at any time. Your credits stack and never expire, so you can build up your analysis balance whenever you want.

Is this better than a traditional home inspection?

OfferWise complements (doesn't replace) home inspections. Use OfferWise to analyze properties BEFORE making offers, then get a physical inspection on your finalist. At $7.50-15 per analysis vs $300-500 per inspection, you can analyze many properties affordably.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor.
