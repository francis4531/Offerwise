The report reads like a briefing from a trusted advisor. The verdict is a sentence, not a number. Issues flow as a timeline from most to least critical. Buyers who are anxious respond better to human language than dashboards. Higher empathy, lower cognitive load.

D

Document-First — White-background, professional grade

The top of the report looks like a real estate document — white background, serif body copy, professional layout. The score and offer are in a sidebar box. Below the fold it shifts back to dark cards. Signals credibility to agents and attorneys. Best if you want the analysis to travel — when a buyer screenshots or prints this to show their agent.
