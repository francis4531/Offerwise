How it works

Four steps. One deliverable. About 90 seconds.

You're already doing the analysis in your head when you walk a client through a disclosure. OfferWise gives you a place to put it on paper — with the cost numbers and offer strategy already filled in.

Step 01

Upload the inspection or disclosure PDF

Drop the file your client just received from the seller or the inspector. OfferWise extracts the findings.

Step 02

Pick a side, enter the budget

Are you representing the buyer or the seller? For buyers, enter their three budget tiers (qualified, comfortable, preferred). For sellers, enter the list price.

Step 03

Write your commentary

Two or three sentences in your own voice — what your client should know about this property, this seller, this market. This is the part that makes the briefing yours.

Step 04

Send to your client

OfferWise generates the one-page briefing and gives you a shareable link. Send from inside OfferWise or copy the link. The email comes from your domain; replies go to you.
