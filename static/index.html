Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first analysis

What if I don't have an inspection report yet?

You can still use OfferWise with just the seller disclosure - we'll analyze what's available. For the most accurate results, we recommend having both documents. You can order an inspection after your offer is accepted (typically $300-500).

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our analysis cross-references your specific documents and applies current repair cost data. It's not a substitute for professional inspection, but it helps you understand what you're looking at and what questions to ask.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept any PDF - including scanned documents, handwritten disclosures, and multi-page inspection reports. Our OCR technology reads even poor-quality scans.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All documents are encrypted in transit (256-bit SSL) and at rest. We delete your uploaded documents after analysis. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I use this for multiple properties?

Absolutely! Many of our users analyze 3-5 properties before making an offer. Our 5-pack ($99) and Investor Pro ($199) plans offer significant savings for multiple analyses.

What if something goes wrong?

If our analysis fails to process your documents due to a technical issue on our end, we'll work with you to resolve it. Contact support@getofferwise.ai and we'll make it right.
