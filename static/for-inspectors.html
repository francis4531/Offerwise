How it works

Three steps. Zero integration.

No API keys, no software partnership required. You can start today.

Step 01

Upload the inspection report

Upload your PDF or paste the text from your completed inspection. We don't keep the PDF file — only the extracted text needed to generate the analysis, stored against the report record so you can re-open it from your dashboard.

Step 02

OfferWise reads your findings

We extract each finding, classify severity, and estimate repair costs. The analysis is attributed to OfferWise — your name stays on the conditions you observed, not the cost numbers.

Step 03

Share a buyer-branded link

Email the link directly from your dashboard or copy it into your own message. The buyer page is headlined with your business name. OfferWise is a tool credit in the footer.

Result

Your buyer has a plan

Each finding has an urgency tag (Safety priority, Pre-close fix, 12-month, 5-year), the specific trade to call (Roofer, Structural Engineer, etc.), and a short note on what the finding means for their offer.
