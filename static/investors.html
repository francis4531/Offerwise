01
Patent-Pending · OfferScore™

The first investability grade for residential real estate.

Every stock has a price-to-earnings ratio. Every bond has a credit rating. Until OfferWise, no residential property had a single, defensible, data-backed investment grade. OfferScore™ synthesizes 50+ factors — structural integrity, disclosure transparency, market position, environmental risk, repair burden, and comparable sale trajectory — into a single score from 0 to 100. It is the first instrument-grade signal ever applied to a homebuying decision at the point of offer.

Patent-pending · cross-document synthesis · 50+ weighted factors · property-specific, not algorithmic estimate
02
Patent-Pending · Seller Transparency Report™

The first systematic cross-reference of disclosure against inspection — at scale.

Sellers are legally required to disclose known defects. They frequently do not. Until OfferWise, there was no automated way to detect the gap between what a seller claimed and what an inspector found. The Seller Transparency Report™ reads both documents, maps every claim in the disclosure against every finding in the inspection report, and surfaces contradictions, omissions, and buried red flags with specific page references. This is not a summarizer. It is a lie detector — patent-pending.

The legal and liability implications are profound: this tool creates documented evidence of disclosure gaps that buyers can use in post-close disputes. No platform has ever offered this.

Patent-pending · disclosure-vs-inspection cross-reference · specific page citations · contradiction scoring
03
Patent-Pending · Property Risk DNA™

A unique risk fingerprint — 12 categories, one visual — for every property on earth.

Risk DNA™ is a composite scoring system that maps a property across 12 simultaneous risk dimensions: structural, roofing, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, moisture, environmental, seismic, wildfire, flood, pest, and financial. No two properties have the same Risk DNA. Unlike a generic inspection checklist, Risk DNA is normalized, comparable across properties, and machine-readable — meaning it can power future portfolio analytics, insurance underwriting, and institutional due diligence tools at scale.

Patent-pending · 12-dimensional composite · comparable across properties · foundation for B2B data licensing
04
Agentic AI Architecture · 13 Specialized Agents

The first multi-agent AI system purpose-built for real estate document intelligence.

OfferWise does not call a single LLM with a long prompt. It deploys a coordinated team of 13 specialized AI agents — each with a defined scope, a specific data source, and a structured output — that run in parallel and synthesize into a single offer strategy. The architecture includes: a document parser agent (OCR + structure extraction), a disclosure cross-reference agent, a market intelligence agent (13 live data sources), a repair cost agent (ZIP-adjusted contractor pricing), a seismic and environmental risk agent (USGS + EPA + FEMA), and an offer architect agent that synthesizes all upstream outputs into three offer tiers plus a ready-to-send addendum.

This is not a chatbot. It is an orchestrated agentic pipeline that produces the same output a team of a buyer's agent, a real estate attorney, a cost estimator, and an environmental consultant would — in 60 seconds.

05
Autonomous Monitoring · OfferWatch™

The first autonomous property intelligence agent that acts without being asked.

After an analysis is complete, OfferWatch™ deploys a persistent monitoring agent on that property. Every day, without user intervention, it checks: listing price against the original asking price (price drop → offer automatically recalculated and delivered), USGS seismic activity within 50km (earthquake → updated structural risk report), county permit records (new permit → undisclosed repair flag), and comparable sale activity (new comps → leverage position updated). It fires alerts to the buyer — and, uniquely, to linked professionals: the inspector, agent, and contractor — the moment anything changes.

This is the first product in residential real estate that is genuinely proactive. It does not wait for the user to return. It monitors the situation and tells everyone who needs to know when the situation changes.

Autonomous · 4 daily monitoring jobs · multi-party notification · idempotent · fires to buyer + inspector + agent + contractor simultaneously
