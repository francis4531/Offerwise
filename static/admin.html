📊 Pricing Analysis

Our strengths: Only platform with per-analysis pricing (no monthly commitment). $29 single is lower commitment than $34.99/mo subscription. Buyer-first positioning is unique — every competitor targets agents.

Pricing risk: DisclosureDuo gives 70 free credits on signup + $34.99/mo for 70 credits. If 1 credit ≈ 1 document, their per-doc price could be as low as ~$0.50/doc. Our $19/analysis is higher per-document if buyers compare naively. But our analysis is a full cross-referenced report with OfferScore, not just a summary.

Recommendation: Consider adding a free first analysis (no credit card) to match competitor onboarding friction. The $19 single, $79/5, $149/12 tier structure is strong for serious buyers who value quality over volume. Keep per-analysis pricing — it aligns with how buyers actually purchase (1-3 homes, not monthly subscription).
