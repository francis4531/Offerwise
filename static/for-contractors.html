How it works

Pre-qualified leads. Zero cold calls.

Every lead comes with the inspection report findings, the repair issue, and the buyer's contact info. You know exactly what they need before you call.

Step 01

Buyer runs an analysis

A homebuyer uploads their inspection report. OfferWise identifies all repair issues and estimates costs.

Step 02

Buyer requests quotes

From their results page, the buyer clicks "Get quotes" on a specific repair item and submits their contact info.

Step 03

We match and notify you

We match the repair type and property ZIP to your trades and service area, then send you the lead via email immediately.

Step 04

You close the job

You call the buyer directly. No middleman, no bidding wars on platforms. Just a warm lead who already knows they need your trade.
